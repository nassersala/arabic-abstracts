## Paper Summary

MobileNets is a highly influential paper that introduced efficient convolutional neural network architectures specifically designed for mobile and embedded vision applications. The key innovation is the use of depthwise separable convolutions that significantly reduce computational cost while maintaining accuracy. The paper introduces two hyperparameters (width multiplier and resolution multiplier) that allow practitioners to tune the model size and speed for their specific use case.

**Impact:** This paper has been highly influential in the mobile AI and edge computing fields, with thousands of citations and widespread adoption in production systems.
