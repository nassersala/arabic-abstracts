## Paper Significance

This is the seminal 1965 paper that introduced the Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) algorithm, one of the most influential algorithms in computational mathematics and signal processing. The paper has been cited over 12,000 times. The algorithm reduces the computational complexity of Fourier series calculation from O(N²) to O(N log N), revolutionizing digital signal processing, communications, and scientific computing.
