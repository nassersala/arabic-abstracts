## Paper Significance

This is one of the most influential papers in generative AI, introducing the foundational concepts for diffusion models that now power:
- Stable Diffusion
- DALL-E 2
- Imagen
- Midjourney
- And many other state-of-the-art image generation systems

The paper achieved breakthrough results on CIFAR-10 (Inception score: 9.46, FID: 3.17) and demonstrated quality comparable to ProgressiveGAN on 256x256 LSUN.
