## Paper Overview
This white paper advocates for better integration of formal methods into computer science and software engineering curricula. It analyzes challenges in teaching formal methods and provides constructive recommendations including improved teaching approaches, systematic integration throughout the curriculum, and a compulsory formal methods course syllabus.

**Significance:** Important educational policy paper addressing the gap between industry needs for formal methods expertise and current university training.
