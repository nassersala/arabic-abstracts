## Paper Overview

This paper presents a methodology for encapsulating formal methods within domain-specific languages (DSLs) to improve industrial adoption. The methodology addresses three key challenges:
1. **Faithful modelling** - ensuring mathematical models accurately represent systems
2. **Scalability** - verification that scales to industrial-sized systems
3. **Accessibility** - making formal methods accessible to domain practitioners

The methodology is demonstrated on the railway signalling domain using the CASL algebraic specification language.
