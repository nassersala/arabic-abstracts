## Paper Significance

XGBoost is one of the most influential machine learning papers of the 2010s, introducing a highly optimized implementation of gradient boosting that became the dominant method for structured/tabular data competitions and real-world applications. The paper won the SIGKDD 2016 Test of Time Award and has been cited over 32,000 times.
