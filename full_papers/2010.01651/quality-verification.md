## Section 4: Results (ICC Analysis)

**Original English:**
To measure the level of agreement among learners, we use the intraclass correlation coefficient (ICC) [18], where each student is considered a rater, the project is the subject of being measured. ICC is widely used in the literature because it is easy to understand, can be used to assess both relative and absolute agreement, and the ability to accommodate a broad array of research scenarios.

**Arabic Translation:**
لقياس مستوى الاتفاق بين المتعلمين، نستخدم معامل الارتباط داخل الفئة (ICC) [18]، حيث يُعتبر كل طالب مُقيماً، والمشروع هو موضوع القياس. يُستخدم ICC على نطاق واسع في الأدبيات لأنه سهل الفهم، ويمكن استخدامه لتقييم كل من الاتفاق النسبي والمطلق، والقدرة على استيعاب مجموعة واسعة من السيناريوهات البحثية.

**Back Translation to English:**
To measure the level of agreement among learners, we use the intraclass correlation coefficient (ICC) [18], where each student is considered a rater, and the project is the subject of measurement. ICC is widely used in the literature because it is easy to understand, can be used to assess both relative and absolute agreement, and has the ability to accommodate a wide range of research scenarios.
