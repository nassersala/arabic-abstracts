## Historical Significance

This paper introduced the B-tree data structure, one of the most important innovations in computer science. B-trees are the foundation for indexing in virtually all modern database systems (MySQL, PostgreSQL, Oracle, SQL Server) and file systems (NTFS, HFS+, ext4). The structure's ability to maintain sorted data and allow searches, insertions, and deletions in logarithmic time while being optimized for block-based storage makes it ideal for disk-based systems.
