## Paper Summary
This SoK (Systematization of Knowledge) paper provides a comprehensive taxonomy of differential privacy variants and extensions. It categorizes all major differential privacy definitions into seven dimensions, establishes ordering relationships between them, and analyzes their properties including composition, post-processing, and convexity.
