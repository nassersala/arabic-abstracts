## Paper Overview

This is a comprehensive survey paper examining Gödel's incompleteness theorems from three perspectives:
1. Classifications of different proofs of Gödel's incompleteness theorems
2. The limit of the applicability of Gödel's first incompleteness theorem (G1)
3. The limit of the applicability of Gödel's second incompleteness theorem (G2)
