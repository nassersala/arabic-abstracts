## Paper Overview

This paper presents a methodology and techniques for formalizing and validating high-level safety-critical requirements, particularly for aerospace, avionics, and railways applications. The work combines first-order, temporal, and hybrid logic with satisfiability procedures based on model checking and satisfiability modulo theory (SMT). The methodology was applied to the European Train Control System (ETCS) requirements validation project.
