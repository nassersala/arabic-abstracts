### English Version

The u-net architecture achieves very good performance on very different biomedical segmentation applications. Thanks to data augmentation with elastic deformations, it only needs very few annotated images and has a very reasonable training time of only 10 hours on a NVidia Titan GPU (6 GB). We provide the full Caffe [6]-based implementation and the trained networks3. We are sure that the u-net architecture can be applied easily to many more tasks.
