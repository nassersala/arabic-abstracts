## Paper Summary

U-Net is a foundational deep learning architecture for biomedical image segmentation. It features:
- A symmetric encoder-decoder structure with skip connections
- Effective training with limited annotated data through data augmentation
- State-of-the-art performance on ISBI challenges
- Fast inference (<1 second for 512x512 images on GPU)

This paper has become one of the most influential works in medical imaging, with thousands of citations and widespread adoption in the biomedical computer vision community.
