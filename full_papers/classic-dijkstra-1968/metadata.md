## Historical Significance

This letter is considered one of the most influential in computer science history:
- Marked the beginning of the structured programming movement
- Spawned the "considered harmful" phrase template used widely in CS
- Editor Niklaus Wirth changed the original title from "A Case Against the Goto Statement"
- Sparked decades of debate about programming language design
