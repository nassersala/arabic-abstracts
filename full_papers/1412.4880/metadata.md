## Paper Overview

This paper describes a pedagogical method for teaching physics using functional programming in Haskell. The author demonstrates how expressing physical concepts in Haskell clarifies the structure of Newtonian mechanics and electromagnetic theory. The paper is organized into sections covering:
1. Introduction to the course and approach
2. Newtonian Mechanics (vectors, state-based approach, numerical methods)
3. Electromagnetic Theory (electric and magnetic fields, type signatures)
4. Conclusion and Future Work
