## Historical Significance

This paper is considered foundational in operating systems scheduling:
- Introduced probabilistic fairness to CPU scheduling
- Influenced modern schedulers (VMware ESX, Linux CFS concepts)
- Pioneered ticket-based resource management
- Solved priority inversion elegantly with ticket transfers
- Extended to I/O bandwidth, memory, and lock management
- Citation count: 1,500+ (Google Scholar)
