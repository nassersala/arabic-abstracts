## Paper Significance

This paper introduced the Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE) algorithm, one of the most important near-term quantum algorithms. VQE is a hybrid quantum-classical algorithm designed to work on noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) devices by drastically reducing coherence time requirements. The algorithm has become foundational for quantum chemistry applications and near-term quantum computing research.
