### English Version

The paper examines three 2-categories and their anti-equivalences: the category of small abelian categories with exact functors, the category of definable additive categories with interpretation functors, and the category of locally coherent abelian categories with coherent morphisms. These frameworks connect algebraic structures, model-theoretic concepts, and geometric perspectives.
