## Paper Overview

This paper presents ZooKeeper, a service for coordinating processes of distributed applications. ZooKeeper is designed to provide a simple and high performance kernel for building more complex coordination primitives at the client. It incorporates elements from group messaging, shared registers, and distributed lock services in a replicated, centralized service.
