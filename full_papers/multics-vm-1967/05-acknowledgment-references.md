### English Version

#### Acknowledgment

The evolution of the concepts presented in this paper represents the efforts of many members of the Multics programming staff. However, the authors wish to express particular appreciation of the work of F.J. Corbató and R.M. Graham in developing the basic design of the Multics Linkage mechanism.
