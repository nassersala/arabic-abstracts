## Paper Importance
This is a foundational paper in authenticated data structures and cryptographic security. It introduces the **path hash accumulator**, a novel cryptographic primitive for efficiently authenticating complex queries on graphs and geometric structures. The paper is highly cited in the fields of:
- Cryptography and security
- Cloud computing and untrusted servers
- Blockchain and distributed systems
- Verifiable computation
- Secure data outsourcing
