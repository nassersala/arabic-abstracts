### English Version

**Full Abstract (from arXiv):**

The problem of elicitation of formal specifications stands as a significant barrier to leveraging formal methods in the development of safety-critical systems. In this paper, we present VISPEC, a graphical tool for the development and visualization of formal specifications by people that do not have training in formal logic. The tool enables users to develop specifications using a graphical formalism which is then automatically translated to Metric Temporal Logic (MTL). We present a usability study which demonstrates that both academics and industry practitioners can develop accurate formal requirements using VISPEC for robotic systems applications including robotic surgery and autonomous quadcopter safety specifications.
