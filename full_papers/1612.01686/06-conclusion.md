### English Version

In this paper, we have presented ongoing work on the use of spatio-temporal models for formal methods-based analysis and testing. We have described different ingredients and their interplay: testing frameworks, TLA+, FocusST and BeSpaceD. The overall goal of our research is the reduction of the impedance mismatch between formal methods and practitioners.
