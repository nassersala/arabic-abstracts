## Paper Overview

This landmark paper introduces Word2Vec, specifically the Continuous Bag-of-Words (CBOW) and Skip-gram models, which revolutionized natural language processing by providing efficient methods to learn high-quality word embeddings from large text corpora. The work demonstrates that meaningful semantic and syntactic relationships can be captured in vector arithmetic operations.
