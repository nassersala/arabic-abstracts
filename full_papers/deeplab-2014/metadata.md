## Paper Summary

This paper introduces DeepLab, a system that combines deep convolutional neural networks (DCNNs) with fully connected Conditional Random Fields (CRFs) for semantic image segmentation. The method addresses the poor localization properties of DCNNs by integrating their outputs with CRFs, achieving 71.6% IOU accuracy on PASCAL VOC-2012 test set and operating at 8 FPS on modern GPUs.
