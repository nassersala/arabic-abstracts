## Paper Overview
WaveNet is a groundbreaking deep neural network for generating raw audio waveforms. It uses a fully probabilistic autoregressive model where each audio sample is conditioned on all previous samples. The model achieved state-of-the-art results in text-to-speech synthesis and demonstrated capability in music generation and phoneme recognition.
