### English Version

In this chapter an overview of distributed systems are presented. The architecture, various characteristics, and the myths on distributed computing are discussed. Further to that, an introduction to client/server systems and World Wide Web are given. The future of distributed computing is still quite uncertain since it is one of many new types of computing. The technology has truly shown its worth as a useful tool for various complex applications.
