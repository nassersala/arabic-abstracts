## Paper Summary

This paper proposes a relaxed privacy definition called Random Differential Privacy (RDP), which requires that adding a randomly drawn new observation to a database has small effect on the output (rather than any arbitrary observation as in standard differential privacy). The paper demonstrates improved accuracy for RDP histograms, particularly for sparse histograms, while maintaining important properties like composition.
