# Applying a Formal Method in Industry: a 25-Year Trajectory
## تطبيق أسلوب رسمي في الصناعة: مسار 25 عاماً

### English Abstract
A 25-year industrial experience report on applying B and Event-B formal methods across railways, smartcards, and automotive domains. The authors explain where and why formal methods succeeded, document tangible benefits, and project future prospects for these formalisms in safety-critical systems.
