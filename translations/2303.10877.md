# Practical Solutions in Fully Homomorphic Encryption -- A Survey Analyzing Existing Acceleration Methods

### English Abstract
This survey examines acceleration methods for fully homomorphic encryption, addressing its computational overhead and storage limitations. The authors investigate research from 2019-2022, classifying acceleration schemes from algorithmic and hardware perspectives. They provide a comprehensive summary of the latest research findings on accelerating FHE and propose evaluation metrics for comparing various approaches, while also discussing future research directions for practical implementation and theoretical advancement in this field.
