# A Manifesto for Applicable Formal Methods
## بيان من أجل أساليب رسمية قابلة للتطبيق

**arXiv ID:** 2112.12758
**Authors:** Mario Gleirscher, Jaco van de Pol, Jim Woodcock
**Year:** 2021
**Categories:** cs.SE, cs.FL, cs.LO
**Translation Quality:** 0.91
**Glossary Terms Used:** validation (التحقق من الصحة)

### English Abstract
This manifesto addresses why formal methods remain underutilized in safety-critical domains despite proven effectiveness. The authors propose principles for achieving mature applicability, asking what constitutes a formal method and what makes it truly applicable from scientific and practical perspectives. Rather than critiquing past work, it aims to promote increased formal methods adoption for maximum benefit.
