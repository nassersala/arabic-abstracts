### English Abstract
This survey examines security threats in large language models across training and deployment phases. The work categorizes various attacks targeting LLMs and explores corresponding defense mechanisms, which are classified as either prevention-based and detection-based defenses. The authors evaluate defense effectiveness against different security threats and aim to provide a structured framework for securing LLMs while highlighting areas needing further research to strengthen protections against emerging challenges.
