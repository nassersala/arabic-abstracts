# A Haskell to FHE Transpiler
## مترجم من Haskell إلى FHE

**arXiv ID:** 2510.08343
**Authors:** Anne Müller, Mohd Kashif, Nico Döttling
**Year:** 2025
**Categories:** cs.CR
**Translation Quality:** 0.90
**Glossary Terms Used:** transpiling, parallelism, cryptographic

### English Abstract
Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) enables the evaluation of programs directly on encrypted data. However, because only basic operations can be performed on ciphertexts, programs must be expressed as boolean or arithmetic circuits. The paper introduces a Haskell-to-FHE transpiler that converts Haskell programs into Boolean circuits for homomorphic evaluation. A second contribution involves automatic circuit parallelization, executing gates in parallel across circuit layers. The authors demonstrate their approach through Private Information Retrieval and AES encryption implementations, achieving evaluation times of 28 seconds with 16 threads and 8 seconds with 100 threads on parallel execution benchmarks.
