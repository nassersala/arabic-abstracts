# QUT: A Unit Testing Framework for Quantum Subroutines

**arXiv ID:** 2509.17538

**Year:** 2025

### English Abstract
We present the architectural design and prototype implementation of QUT (Quantum Unit Testing), a framework for unit testing of quantum subroutines. The framework emphasizes usability through polymorphic probabilistic assertions that adapt based on input data types - which may be qubit measurement outcomes, density matrices, or Choi matrices. The system integrates multiple testing protocols including quantum process tomography and quantum state tomography, built on the Qiskit stack for compatibility with various quantum hardware backends. We rely on the reasoning provided by the denotational semantics of quantum subroutines to establish testing protocols, highlighting the differences between quantum and classical unit testing.
