# Contextually indexed contextual categories
## الفئات السياقية المفهرسة سياقياً

**arXiv ID:** 1809.03002
**Authors:** Valery Isaev
**Year:** 2018
**Categories:** math.CT, math.AT
**Translation Quality:** 0.91
**Glossary Terms Used:** category, proof

### English Abstract
The paper introduces a generalization of indexed and contextual categories called contextually indexed contextual categories, which serve as models for indexed type theories (as opposed to ordinary type theories). The author also defines type-theoretic semi-fibration categories that extend type-theoretic fibration categories, establishing that any model category with pullback-stable cofibrations qualifies as such a structure. The work demonstrates that type-theoretic semi-fibration categories yield contextually indexed contextual categories possessing finite limits. Additionally, the paper proves that simplicial sets with the Joyal model structure form a locally small Cartesian closed contextually indexed contextual category supporting indexed limits and colimits.
