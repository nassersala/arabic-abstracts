# Exploring the Theory and Practice of Concurrency in the Entity-Component-System Pattern

### English Abstract
The paper develops a formal model called Core ECS to rigorously understand the Entity-Component-System pattern. The researchers identify a class of programs that "behave deterministically regardless of scheduling," positioning ECS as a potential deterministic concurrent programming model. Their analysis of real-world ECS frameworks reveals unexploited opportunities for deterministic concurrency, suggesting new implementation techniques could better leverage these opportunities.
