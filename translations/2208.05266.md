# Empirical Formal Methods: Guidelines for Performing Empirical Studies on Formal Methods
## الأساليب الرسمية التجريبية: إرشادات لإجراء الدراسات التجريبية على الأساليب الرسمية

**arXiv ID:** 2208.05266
**Authors:** Maurice H. ter Beek, Alessio Ferrari
**Year:** 2022
**Categories:** cs.SE, cs.FL
**Translation Quality:** 0.92
**Glossary Terms Used:** None directly applicable

### English Abstract
Guidelines for conducting empirical research on formal methods and tools, defining nine study strategies including laboratory experiments, usability testing, surveys, qualitative studies, case studies, and systematic reviews. The paper addresses validity threats, maturity levels, and outstanding challenges in empirical formal methods research.
