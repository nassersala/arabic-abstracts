# A Mathematical Model, Implementation and Study of a Swarm System
## نموذج رياضي وتطبيق ودراسة لنظام سربي

**arXiv ID:** 1310.2279
**Authors:** Blesson Varghese, Gerard McKee
**Year:** 2013
**Categories:** cs.RO
**Translation Quality:** 0.91
**Glossary Terms Used:** algorithm (implied)

### English Abstract
The researchers develop mathematical models for swarm systems using macroscopic primitives. They propose pattern formation and transformation methods including macroscopic and mathematical transformation approaches. The work formally expresses transformation problems and examines four specialized cases, presenting simulations validating feasibility and comparing the two transformation methodologies.
