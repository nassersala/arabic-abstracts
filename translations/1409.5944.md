### English Abstract
The paper presents an accessible middle-ground exposition of Gödel's first incompleteness theorem. It avoids both hand-waving philosophical discussions and the heavy machinery of traditional mathematical logic, instead offering a short, self-contained rigorous proof suitable for undergraduate audiences without requiring specialized formal logic background.
