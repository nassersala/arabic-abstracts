### English Abstract
This paper examines OpenAI's Sora, a text-to-video generative model released in February 2024. The authors provide an overview of the model's technical foundations, tracing its development and investigating the underlying technologies that enable it to simulate physical worlds. They explore practical applications across industries like filmmaking and education, while also addressing critical challenges including safety and bias mitigation. The review concludes by discussing future development paths for video generation technology and its potential to enhance human-AI collaboration.
