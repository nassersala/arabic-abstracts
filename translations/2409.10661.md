### English Abstract
This work examines parallel computing approaches for high-performance systems. The paper discusses three main implementation strategies: multithreading on CPUs, GPU and accelerator devices, and SIMD architectures. The author addresses challenges in optimizing parallel programs, including data transfer between hosts and devices in heterogeneous systems that create performance limitations.
