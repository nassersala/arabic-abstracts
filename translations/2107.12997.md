# Fully Homomorphically Encrypted Deep Learning as a Service
## التعلم العميق المشفر بالتشفير المتماثل الكامل كخدمة

**arXiv ID:** 2107.12997
**Authors:** George Onoufriou, Paul Mayfield, Georgios Leontidis
**Year:** 2021
**Categories:** cs.LG, cs.CR
**Translation Quality:** 0.95
**Glossary Terms Used:** homomorphic encryption, deep learning, privacy, implementation, security, algorithm

### English Abstract
This research explores how Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) can enable computation of both addition and multiplication while preserving privacy in deep learning applications. The team investigates implementation challenges, proposes solutions, and demonstrates the approach through milk yield prediction. Their findings indicate that while FHE requires substantial memory resources, computational timing remains reasonable, enabling absolutely private predictions without compromising model functionality or data security.
