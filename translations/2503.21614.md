### English Abstract
This survey examines recent Large Reasoning Models like DeepSeek-R1 and OpenAI o1, which "scale up the length of Chain-of-Thought reasoning during inference." The authors identify critical inefficiencies, including redundant content and unnecessary analysis. They provide "a comprehensive overview of recent efforts aimed at improving reasoning efficiency" across the model lifecycle, from pretraining through inference, addressing practical constraints in agent-based systems and deployment scenarios.
