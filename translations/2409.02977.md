# Large Language Model-Based Agents for Software Engineering: A Survey

**Year:** 2024
**Categories:** cs.SE, cs.AI

### English Abstract
The recent advancement in Large Language Models has introduced a new paradigm of AI agents called LLM-based agents. These agents extend the capabilities of standalone LLMs by "enhancing LLMs with the capabilities of perceiving and utilizing external resources and tools." Multi-agent systems combined with human interaction offer potential for addressing complex software engineering challenges. The survey examines 106 papers analyzed from both software engineering and agent perspectives. The authors present "a comprehensive and systematic survey on LLM-based agents for SE," discussing open challenges and future research directions. A repository of the papers reviewed is available at the project's GitHub location.
