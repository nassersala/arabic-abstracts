### English Abstract
The paper introduces a comprehensive sensor dataset designed for autonomous vehicle development. It contains 1000 scenes of 20 seconds each, captured with six cameras, five radars, and one lidar providing complete 360-degree coverage. The dataset includes "full 3D bounding boxes for 23 classes and 8 attributes," with substantially more annotations and imagery compared to predecessor datasets. The authors establish novel evaluation metrics for detection and tracking tasks, offer dataset analysis, and supply baseline implementations for lidar and camera-based detection and tracking systems, with all resources made publicly available.
