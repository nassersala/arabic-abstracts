# SVP_p is NP-Hard for all p > 2, Even to Approximate Within a Factor of 2^{log^{1-ε} n}
## مسألة SVP_p صعبة من فئة NP لجميع p > 2، حتى عند التقريب ضمن عامل 2^{log^{1-ε} n}

**arXiv ID:** 2511.04125
**Authors:** Isaac M. Hair, Amit Sahai
**Year:** 2025
**Categories:** Computational Complexity (cs.CC)
**Translation Quality:** 0.89
**Glossary Terms Used:** algorithm, complexity, proof, matrix

### English Abstract
The paper establishes that SVP_p is NP-hard to approximate within a factor of 2^{log^{1 - ε} n}, for all constants ε > 0 and p > 2. This represents the first proof of NP-hardness for exact SVP_p in finite ℓ_p norms. The authors employ elementary proof techniques, reducing from regularized PCP instances using gadgets based on Vandermonde and Hadamard matrices.
