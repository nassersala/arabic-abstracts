# Current research on Gödel's incompleteness theorems
## البحوث الحالية حول نظريات عدم الاكتمال لغودل

**arXiv ID:** 2009.04887
**Authors:** Yong Cheng
**Year:** 2020
**Categories:** Mathematics > Logic (math.LO)
**Translation Quality:** 0.94
**Glossary Terms Used:** theorem, proof, incompleteness, applicability, classification

### English Abstract
A survey examining Gödel's incompleteness theorems from three perspectives: classifications of different proofs of Gödel's incompleteness theorems, the limit of the applicability of Gödel's first incompleteness theorem, and the limit of the applicability of Gödel's second incompleteness theorem.
