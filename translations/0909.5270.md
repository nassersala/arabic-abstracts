# Strictification of categories weakly enriched in symmetric monoidal categories
## تصريم الفئات المُثرَى ضعيفاً في الفئات الأحادية المتماثلة

**arXiv ID:** 0909.5270
**Authors:** Bertrand Guillou
**Year:** 2009
**Categories:** math.CT, math.KT
**Translation Quality:** 0.92
**Glossary Terms Used:** category

### English Abstract
The paper presents two approaches demonstrating that "categories weakly enriched over symmetric monoidal categories can be strictified to categories enriched in permutative categories." The work addresses a generalized version of strictification for bimonoidal structures, extending the concept to handle multiple objects. This theoretical contribution advances understanding of categorical enrichment by showing how weaker compositional structures can be transformed into stricter, more rigid forms while preserving essential properties.
