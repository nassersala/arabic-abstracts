# Formal Synthesis of Controllers for Safety-Critical Autonomous Systems: Developments and Challenges

### English Abstract
This review examines formal controller synthesis for safety-critical autonomous systems across deterministic, non-deterministic, and stochastic models. Coverage includes abstraction-based and abstraction-free methods, data-driven synthesis integration, multi-agent system approaches, and scalability solutions for large-scale systems.
