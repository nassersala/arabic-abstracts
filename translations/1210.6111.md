### English Abstract
This work addresses validation in model-driven engineering contexts. The authors present methodology for validating transformations involving the Ontology Definition Meta-model and OWL. Their approach uses logic programming to verify both syntactic and semantic requirements, including pre/post conditions and invariants. They demonstrate the technique using Entity-Relationship to Relational Model transformation as an example.
