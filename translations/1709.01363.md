### English Abstract
The paper examines stream processing engines and resource elasticity mechanisms in cloud computing environments. It focuses on how systems can dynamically scale resources based on varying demands—a particularly complex challenge when combining edge and cloud computing. The survey addresses continuous data streams must be processed under very short delays in contexts such as smart cities, infrastructure monitoring, and Internet of Things applications.
