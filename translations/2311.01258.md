### English Abstract
This monograph surveys formal methods applications in autonomous systems, covering correct-by-construction synthesis, probabilistic settings, and systems with learning. Topics include handling uncertainty, monitoring-based synthesis, and overcoming formal methods limitations through learning integration, concluding with future directions.
