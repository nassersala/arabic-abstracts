### English Abstract
The research demonstrates how cryptographically-authenticated data structures can be used to protect against attacks based on memory corruption through hardware-assisted cryptographic mechanisms like Intel's AES-NI and ARM's Pointer Authentication. The authors present implementations of secure stacks and queues with minimal overall performance overhead (3.4%-6.4% slowdown) in OpenCV performance tests, accompanied by correctness proofs. The work was presented at the 2022 IEEE Secure Development Conference.
