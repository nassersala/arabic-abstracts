### English Abstract
The paper proposes a paradigm shift in computational system design by introducing an innovative data-centric paradigm that replaces conventional node-centric approaches. The model employs a multimodal approach that incorporates objects, events, concepts, and actions and establishes an ontology to promote semantic consistency and secure data handling across distributed ecosystems.
