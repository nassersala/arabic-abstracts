# SoK: Fully Homomorphic Encryption Compilers
## SoK: مترجمات التشفير المتماثل الكامل

**arXiv ID:** 2101.07078
**Authors:** Alexander Viand, Patrick Jattke, Anwar Hithnawi
**Year:** 2021
**Categories:** cs.CR
**Translation Quality:** 0.92
**Glossary Terms Used:** homomorphic encryption, compiler, security, privacy, implementation

### English Abstract
This systematization of knowledge examines FHE tools and compilers. The authors note that Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) allows a third party to perform arbitrary computations on encrypted data, learning neither the inputs nor the computation results. The paper surveys current FHE tools, conducts experimental evaluations across various applications, and provides recommendations for developers building FHE-based systems. The research was accepted for publication at IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy 2021.
