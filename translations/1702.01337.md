# Descent and Galois theory for Hopf categories
## نظرية النزول وجالوا لفئات هوبف

**arXiv ID:** 1702.01337
**Authors:** S. Caenepeel, T. Fieremans
**Year:** 2017
**Categories:** math.RA, math.CT
**Translation Quality:** 0.93
**Glossary Terms Used:** category, framework, proof

### English Abstract
The authors develop descent theory applicable to linear categories, establishing that when a linear category extends a diagonal category with appropriate flatness conditions, the descent data category is isomorphic to the representation category of the diagonal category. They then extend this framework to Hopf-Galois descent theory for linear Hopf categories, introducing the concept of Hopf-Galois category extensions. The paper also presents a dual theory examining actions by dual linear Hopf categories on linear categories, demonstrating that Hopf-Galois category extensions over groupoid algebras correspond to strongly graded linear categories.
