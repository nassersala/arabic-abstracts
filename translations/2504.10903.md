# Efficient Reasoning Models: A Survey

### English Abstract
The paper addresses computational efficiency in reasoning models that generate extended Chain-of-Thoughts. The authors organize solutions into three categories: (1) "shorter" - condensing lengthy reasoning chains into concise versions; (2) "smaller" - building compact models through knowledge distillation and compression; and (3) "faster" - implementing efficient decoding strategies. A curated resource collection is maintained in a GitHub repository.
