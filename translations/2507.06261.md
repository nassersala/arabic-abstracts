### English Abstract
The Gemini 2.X model family represents breakthrough advances in AI capabilities. Gemini 2.5 Pro is the most capable model yet, achieving state-of-the-art performance on frontier coding and reasoning benchmarks. The system can process up to 3 hours of video content, enabling comprehensive analysis of extended visual material alongside text and audio inputs. The family's unique combination of long context, multimodal and reasoning capabilities unlocks new agentic workflows for autonomous, complex task execution. Gemini 2.5 Flash provides excellent reasoning abilities at a fraction of the compute and latency requirements of the Pro version. The collective model family spans the full Pareto frontier of model capability versus cost, allowing developers to balance performance against computational efficiency for different applications.
