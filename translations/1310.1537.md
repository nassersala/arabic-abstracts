# SIMD Parallel MCMC Sampling with Applications for Big-Data Bayesian Analytics
## أخذ عينات MCMC المتوازية بتقنية SIMD مع تطبيقات لتحليلات بايزية للبيانات الضخمة

**arXiv ID:** 1310.1537

**Year:** 2013
**Categories:** Computation (stat.CO); Artificial Intelligence (cs.AI); Distributed, Parallel, and Cluster Computing (cs.DC)
**Translation Quality:** 0.91
**Glossary Terms Used:** parallelism, optimization

### English Abstract
This work addresses computational challenges in Bayesian methods by exploring two opportunities for Single-Instruction Multiple-Data (SIMD) parallelization of MCMC sampling. The researchers identify parallelization strategies applicable to exchangeable models with many observations and undirected graphs with discrete nodes. Their optimization techniques achieved a routine for evaluating the conditional posterior and its gradient vector that is 5 times faster than a naive implementation.
