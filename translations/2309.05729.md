# A Note on the Incompleteness of Gödel's Incompleteness Theorems

### English Abstract
The paper examines how automated theorem provers that enumerate theorems recursively may fail to identify certain valid theorems through a mechanism analogous to Gödel's incompleteness proof. The author notes this observation has pedagogical value for students learning about recursive enumeration in automated theorem proving, though it lacks significant practical implications.
