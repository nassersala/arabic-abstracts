# Lattices, Homomorphic Encryption, and CKKS
## الشبكات البلورية، والتشفير المتماثل، وCKKS

**arXiv ID:** 2205.03511
**Authors:** Vir Pathak
**Year:** 2022
**Categories:** cs.CR
**Translation Quality:** 0.93
**Glossary Terms Used:** homomorphic encryption, cryptographic, security, algorithm

### English Abstract
This work surveys lattice-based cryptography and homomorphic encryption topics. It covers lattice problems including LWE and RLWE with associated reductions, provides comprehensive treatment of the CKKS homomorphic encryption scheme, and includes worked examples. The author acknowledges that CKKS has been proven to have some vulnerabilities due to an attack from Micciancio and Li and does not satisfy security definitions proposed for approximate encryption schemes.
