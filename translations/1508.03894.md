# An experimental Study using ACSL and Frama-C to formulate and verify Low-Level Requirements from a DO-178C compliant Avionics Project
## دراسة تجريبية باستخدام ACSL و Frama-C لصياغة والتحقق من المتطلبات منخفضة المستوى من مشروع إلكترونيات طيران متوافق مع DO-178C

**arXiv ID:** 1508.03894
**Authors:** Frank Dordowsky
**Year:** 2015
**Categories:** cs.SE, cs.LO
**Translation Quality:** 0.91
**Glossary Terms Used:** validation (التحقق من الصحة)

### English Abstract
Reports on a case study by ESG examining formal verification's application to avionics software. The study formalized specifications in ACSL and verified source code using Frama-C with the WP plugin. Evaluates practical criteria for SME industrial adoption and identifies challenges specific to embedded real-time programming.
