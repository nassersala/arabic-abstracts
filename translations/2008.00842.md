### English Abstract
The survey examines stream processing as an active research field spanning over two decades. It provides a comprehensive overview of fundamental aspects of stream processing systems and their evolution across key functional areas including out-of-order data management, state management, fault tolerance, high availability, load management, elasticity, and reconfiguration.
