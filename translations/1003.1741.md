# Formalization and Validation of Safety-Critical Requirements
## الصياغة الرسمية والتحقق من المتطلبات الحرجة من حيث السلامة

**arXiv ID:** 1003.1741
**Authors:** Alessandro Cimatti, Marco Roveri, Angelo Susi, Stefano Tonetta
**Year:** 2010
**Categories:** cs.SE
**Translation Quality:** 0.92
**Glossary Terms Used:** validation (التحقق من الصحة)

### English Abstract
This work develops methodology and techniques for formalizing and validating high-level safety-critical requirements, particularly for aerospace, avionics, and railways. Using an expressive language combining first-order, temporal, and hybrid logic with satisfiability procedures based on model checking and satisfiability modulo theory, authors apply their approach to railway requirements validation.
